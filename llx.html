Learners' Lodge

Learner's Lodge was one of my big projects when I was in Grade 12. Fuelled by my passion for learning, I wanted to explore and share what psychology and neuroscience tell us in how to learn the most effectively. Funnily enough, I learned that teaching something is one of the best ways to learn, so it seems like I made the right decision.

Here, you will find the various lessons that I compiled along with the Learners' Lodge Exec Team. Currently still work in progress.

All the art work is courtesy of @eri.lw, whom was a member of the Exec Team.
